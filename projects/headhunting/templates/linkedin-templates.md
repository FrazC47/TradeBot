# 💼 LinkedIn Outreach Templates

## Connection Request + Note

### Version 1: Direct Opportunity

Hi [First Name], I'm a talent partner working with [Company] on a [Role] search. Your background in [specific skill] caught my attention. Would love to connect and share more details!

---

### Version 2: Compliment + Soft Ask

Hi [First Name], really impressed by your work on [specific project/post]. I'm helping [Company] grow their [team] and would love to add you to my network. No immediate ask - just connecting with great talent!

---

### Version 3: Mutual Interest

Hi [First Name], I saw your post about [topic] - really insightful! I'm working in the [industry] space and would love to connect with folks who think deeply about this stuff.

---

## InMail Message (after connecting)

Hi [First Name],

Thanks for connecting! I'm currently helping [Company] hire for a [Role] and your background stood out.

**Quick context:**
- [Company stage/size]
- [Interesting technical challenge or product]
- [Compensation range if appropriate]

Are you open to new opportunities, or should I keep you in mind for future roles?

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Response Templates

### Interested Response

Great to hear! When would be a good time for a quick 15-20 minute call? I'd love to learn more about what you're looking for and share details about the role.

[Calendar link if you have one]

### Not Interested Response

Totally understand! Would you mind if I keep you in mind for future opportunities? Also, if you know anyone who might be a good fit, I'd appreciate any referrals.

### "Not now, maybe later"

No problem at all! Mind if I check back in [timeframe]? In the meantime, feel free to connect with me here so we can stay in touch.
