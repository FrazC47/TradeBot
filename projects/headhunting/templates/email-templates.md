I came across your profile and was impressed by your work on [specific project/achievement]. I'm currently helping [Company] build out their [team/department], and your background in [skill/domain] seems like a strong fit.

**About the role:**
- [Key highlight 1]
- [Key highlight 2]
- [Key highlight 3]

Would you be open to a brief conversation to learn more? No pressure if now isn't the right time.

Best,
[Your Name]
